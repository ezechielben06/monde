# **App Name**: MondeTransfo

## Core Features:

- User Onboarding & Profile Creation: Allow users to register, create their personal wellness profile with detailed preferences, health metrics, and initial transformation goals.
- Personalized Dashboard: Display a tailored dashboard showcasing the user's current wellness plan across beauty, sport, nutrition, and coaching, along with progress summaries and upcoming appointments.
- Progress Tracking & Logging: Enable users to easily log their workouts, meals, meditation sessions, or mood, and visualize their transformation journey with interactive charts and metrics over time.
- Service Booking & Scheduling: Allow users to browse and book available services such as beauty treatments, fitness classes, and personalized coaching sessions directly through the app, with real-time availability.
- Subscription & Payment Management: Users can view their active global transformation subscriptions, manage automatic payments securely, access their payment history, and upgrade or downgrade their plans.
- AI Wellness Recommender Tool: An AI-powered tool that analyzes user progress and preferences to provide personalized daily recommendations for optimal activities, healthy recipes, mindfulness exercises, or targeted coaching insights.
- Wellness Resource Library: Provide a curated, easily searchable library of articles, video tutorials, and guides on beauty, fitness, nutrition, and personal development topics to support continuous user learning and growth.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep and vibrant blue (#2929CC). This shade represents technological sophistication, trust, and the profound depth of personal transformation. Its richness provides excellent contrast for interactive elements against a light background.
- Background color: An extremely light, desaturated blue-gray (#F4F5FB). This choice offers a clean, serene, and expansive canvas, reducing visual clutter and allowing core content and progress visuals to prominently stand out.
- Accent color: A rich and dynamic purple (#7300B3). Selected for its vibrancy and association with creativity, spirituality, and aspiration, this color is ideal for compelling call-to-action buttons, key highlights, and notification indicators.
- Headlines will feature 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) to convey a modern, slightly technical, yet clean and impactful aesthetic, ensuring immediate attention. Body text will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its superior readability, objective tone, and versatility, providing comfort for detailed descriptions and personalized content.
- Implement sleek, minimalist line icons throughout the interface. This style reinforces the app's modern, digital, and integrated wellness theme, ensuring clarity, elegance, and quick comprehension of all functionalities.
- Design with a clean, card-based layout featuring a clear hierarchy and generous white space. This approach enhances readability, intuitively guides users through their personalized plans, and efficiently presents progress. Emphasize a mobile-first responsive design.
- Integrate subtle, fluid transitions and purposeful micro-interactions for elements such as progress updates, form submissions, and seamless navigation. This delivers a sophisticated, responsive, and highly engaging user experience.
